About Me

My name is Viviana Arenas, and I was born and raised in New York City. I have a previous background in Art Therapy, receiving a Master's from New York University in May 2018, and have been practicing ever since. It's been a fruitful experience that has enhanced my ability to communicate through both verbal and artistic means.

I am currently enrolled in a Full Stack Development course from Columbia University, establishing skills in HTMl, CSS, Javascript, and so much more that I have yet to learn! My main reasons for learning Coding is to embrace computer programming language to effectively problem-solve challenges, apply technical skills and knowledge to any project, and most importantly, to understand the technology that I was intimidated by. As I continue my studies, I embrace any learning opportunities, as frustrating as they may be, for in that way I know I am growing as a coder.

Currently resides in Brooklyn, NY with her husband. Spare time includes watching animated TV shows, eating great food, and going for walks along the water.
